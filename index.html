About Me

Education: I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate in Astronomy at the Nicolaus Copernicus Astronomical Center in Warsaw. Prior to this I was project research assistant at the National Centre for Radio Astrophysics. I completed my BS-MS dual degree from the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER), Pune.

Research Interests: Classical Pulsators, Stellar Evolution and Pulsation modeling–MESA code, Variable stars in binary systems, Asteroseismology, and Stellar nucleosynthesis.
